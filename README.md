# Informed Consent Decision Brief

A plain-English decision support tool for clinical trial participants — and any complex informed consent decision.

Built by the **Academy of Life Planning** as a prototype for the [Total Wealth Planning™](https://aolp.co.uk) movement.

## What it does

Takes a long, dense patient information sheet and converts it into:
- A plain-English **decision brief** — what actually matters for your decision
- A structured **options comparison** — honest upsides and downsides
- A **multi-factor scoring tool** — so you can weigh options against what matters most to you

This is informed consent as it should be: **agency in, not just signature out.**

## How to use

1. Paste your patient information sheet into the text area
2. Click "Generate my decision brief"
3. Read the brief, compare options, and use the scoring tool to clarify your thinking
4. Take your brief to your clinical team discussion

## Tech

Single-file HTML/CSS/JS app. Uses the Anthropic Claude API (claude-sonnet-4) to extract and structure the information sheet.

## Deployment

Deployed via Vercel. No database, no backend, no data stored.

## Disclaimer

This tool supports informed decision-making. It does not provide medical advice. Always discuss decisions with your clinical team.
